our services

1- Space Communications Services

We offer a comprehensive range of satellite communication services that include the following:
1-Supply and installation of all types of fixed and mobile terminal stations.
2-Providing technical support, maintenance, and training services.
3-Providing various satellite capacities to meet all your needs.
4-Providing encryption systems for satellite communications.

2- Remote Sensing Services

We provide accurate and advanced remote sensing services, offering space images of various resolutions and types, such as (SAR, Multispectral, and Hyperspectral). We offer solutions for processing satellite images and specialized software for tracking and simulating satellite paths, in addition to specialized training courses.

3- Building Ground Stations and Network and Satellite Control Centers

We specialize in building advanced receiving and transit stations for communication and remote sensing satellites, as well as building Network Operations Centers (NOC) and Satellite Control Centers (SCC), including infrastructure and software provision. We also provide exceptional maintenance services and integrated ground connectivity between stations.

4- Consultancy Services in the Field of Satellites

We offer specialized consultations in the design and manufacturing of small and medium satellites, overseeing all design stages and environmental testing. We support our clients with cleanroom setups, frequency hopping systems, and satellite launch services, along with necessary insurance provisions.
